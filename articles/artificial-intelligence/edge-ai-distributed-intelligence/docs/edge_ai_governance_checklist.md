# Edge AI and Distributed Intelligence Governance Checklist

Recommended governance questions:

1. What decisions occur at the device, edge, and cloud layers?
2. Which nodes are safety-critical?
3. What latency budget applies to each edge decision?
4. What data remains local?
5. What data or metadata is transmitted?
6. Are edge models versioned and documented?
7. Are model updates signed and controlled?
8. Are compromised nodes detectable?
9. Is secure aggregation required?
10. Is differential privacy required?
11. Are fallback and fail-safe modes defined?
12. Are node ownership and operational responsibility documented?
13. Are edge decisions auditable?
14. Are cyber-physical risks assessed?
15. Is there an incident response plan for distributed failures?
