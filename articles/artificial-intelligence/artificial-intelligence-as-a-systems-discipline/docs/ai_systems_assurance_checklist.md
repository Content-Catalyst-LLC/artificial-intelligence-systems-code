# AI Systems Assurance Checklist

## System Definition

- System purpose is documented.
- Intended use is documented.
- Prohibited use is documented.
- Human and institutional decision roles are documented.
- Affected stakeholders are identified.

## Data and Measurement

- Data sources are documented.
- Measurement validity is reviewed.
- Data provenance is preserved.
- Privacy and consent requirements are reviewed.
- Bias and representativeness are assessed.

## Model and Evaluation

- Model purpose and version are documented.
- Validation evidence is preserved.
- Calibration is reviewed.
- Robustness is tested.
- Subgroup performance is reviewed.
- Uncertainty is communicated.

## Infrastructure and Monitoring

- Deployment architecture is documented.
- Monitoring coverage is defined.
- Drift detection is implemented.
- Security controls are reviewed.
- Incident escalation is documented.

## Human and Workflow Integration

- User interface is reviewed.
- Human oversight rules are defined.
- Override and escalation are available.
- Contestability process is documented.
- User reliance is monitored.

## Governance

- Risk owner is assigned.
- Governance review cadence is defined.
- Audit artifacts are preserved.
- Regulatory obligations are reviewed.
- Retirement criteria are documented.
