# Model Training, Optimization, and Evaluation

This article folder supports the article "Model Training, Optimization, and Evaluation" in the Artificial Intelligence Systems knowledge series.

It includes reproducible examples for:

- train/validation/test splitting
- empirical risk minimization
- optimization trajectories
- learning curves
- classification metrics
- probability calibration
- grouped diagnostics
- distribution shift monitoring
- SQL metadata for training and evaluation systems
- advanced Jupyter notebooks

The examples are educational and synthetic unless otherwise documented.
