# AI Governance Control Catalog

Suggested control categories:

1. Documentation
2. Data governance
3. Model evaluation
4. Bias and fairness review
5. Human oversight
6. Explainability and transparency
7. Security and misuse controls
8. Privacy and data protection
9. Monitoring and drift detection
10. Incident reporting
11. Vendor management
12. Legal and regulatory review
13. Decommissioning and retirement

Each control should map to:
- control owner
- required evidence
- applicable risk tier
- review frequency
- escalation threshold
