# Scientific AI Governance Checklist

## Research Purpose

- Research question is documented.
- Scientific domain and scope are documented.
- Approved AI uses are defined.
- Prohibited uses are defined.

## Data and Provenance

- Dataset sources are documented.
- Measurement methods are documented.
- Data versions are preserved.
- Known limitations are recorded.
- Licensing and access constraints are documented.

## Model and Simulation

- Model architecture or method is documented.
- Hyperparameters are recorded.
- Random seeds are preserved.
- Simulation versions are documented.
- Surrogate model validity range is defined.

## Validation

- Held-out validation is complete.
- Out-of-distribution testing is reviewed.
- Physical or domain plausibility is checked.
- Experimental validation plan is documented.
- Reproducibility check is complete.

## Governance

- Human reviewer is assigned.
- Hypothesis status is tracked.
- Causal claims are explicitly marked.
- Uncertainty is communicated.
- Publication or release constraints are reviewed.
