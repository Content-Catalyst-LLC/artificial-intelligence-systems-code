# Synthetic Content Policy Template

## Purpose

Define how generative AI may be used to create, edit, transform, summarize, or publish content.

## Approved Uses

- Drafting support
- Ideation
- Internal prototypes
- Research assistance with source verification
- Design exploration
- Code scaffolding with review

## Prohibited Uses

- Undisclosed synthetic impersonation
- Fabricated evidence
- Unreviewed high-stakes advice
- Synthetic content presented as verified reporting without review
- Content that violates legal, privacy, or safety requirements

## Review Requirements

- Human review for external publication
- Fact-checking for knowledge claims
- Legal review for sensitive domains
- Provenance metadata for published synthetic assets
- Disclosure where required by policy or law

## Logging Requirements

- Prompt or prompt hash
- Model name and version
- Output hash
- Editor or reviewer
- Publication decision
- Disclosure status
