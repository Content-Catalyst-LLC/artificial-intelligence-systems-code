# Robustness and Adversarial Resilience Governance Checklist

Recommended governance questions:

1. What threat models have been documented?
2. What attacker capabilities are assumed?
3. What attacker knowledge is assumed?
4. Which lifecycle stages are in scope?
5. What perturbation budgets were tested?
6. Which attack methods were used?
7. Were adaptive attacks considered?
8. Were corruption and distribution-shift tests performed?
9. Were physical-world tests needed?
10. Was clean accuracy compared to robust accuracy?
11. Was robustness evaluated by subgroup or scenario?
12. Are runtime anomaly signals monitored?
13. Is there a fallback or rollback procedure?
14. Who owns robustness incidents?
15. How often are robustness tests repeated?
