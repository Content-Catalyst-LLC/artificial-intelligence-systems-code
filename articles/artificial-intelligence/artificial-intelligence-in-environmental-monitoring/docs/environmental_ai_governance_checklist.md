# Environmental AI Governance Checklist

## Monitoring Purpose

- Environmental purpose is documented.
- Affected ecosystems and communities are identified.
- Approved uses and prohibited uses are documented.
- Human review requirements are defined.

## Data and Measurement

- Sensor inventory is maintained.
- Calibration status is tracked.
- Remote-sensing product assumptions are documented.
- Missingness and spatial coverage gaps are reviewed.
- Environmental justice monitoring gaps are identified.

## Model Validation

- Spatial validation is completed.
- Temporal validation is completed.
- Event-based validation is completed.
- Calibration is reviewed.
- Uncertainty is documented.
- Out-of-distribution conditions are tested.

## Alerts and Intervention

- Alert thresholds are documented.
- False alarms and missed events are reviewed.
- Verification procedures are defined.
- Escalation and public notification rules are documented.

## Governance

- Data lineage is preserved.
- Model versions are recorded.
- Review decisions are auditable.
- Community contestability is considered.
- Environmental and public-health implications are reviewed.
