# Cyber-Physical Assurance Checklist

## System Definition

- Infrastructure domain is documented.
- Physical assets are mapped.
- Digital dependencies are mapped.
- Control actions are documented.
- Human operator roles are documented.

## Data and Sensors

- Sensor inventory exists.
- Sensor calibration status is tracked.
- Data quality checks are implemented.
- Telemetry latency is monitored.
- Missing data handling is documented.

## Models and Digital Twins

- Digital twin scope is documented.
- Calibration and uncertainty are documented.
- Model limitations are documented.
- Approved and prohibited uses are defined.

## Reliability and Resilience

- Failure modes are identified.
- Cascading failure scenarios are tested.
- Degraded-mode operation is defined.
- Human override is available.
- Incident response is documented.

## Governance

- Public purpose is documented.
- Equity review is included.
- Cybersecurity review is included.
- Procurement and vendor risks are reviewed.
- Community impact and contestability are considered.
