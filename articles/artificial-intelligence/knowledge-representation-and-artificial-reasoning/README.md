# Knowledge Representation and Artificial Reasoning

This article folder supports the article "Knowledge Representation and Artificial Reasoning" in the Artificial Intelligence Systems knowledge series.

It includes reproducible examples for:

- symbolic facts and triples
- rule-based inference
- forward chaining
- transitive subclass reasoning
- knowledge graph intuition
- uncertainty and belief diagnostics
- reasoning coverage analysis
- SQL metadata for knowledge bases, ontologies, inference runs, and governance review
- advanced Jupyter notebooks

The examples are educational and synthetic unless otherwise documented.
