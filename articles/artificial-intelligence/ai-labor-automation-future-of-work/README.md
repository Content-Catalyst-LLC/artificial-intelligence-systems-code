# AI, Labor, Automation, and the Future of Work

This repository folder supports the Sustainable Catalyst article:

**AI, Labor, Automation, and the Future of Work**

It provides reproducible examples for task-exposure scoring, automation-versus-augmentation analysis, job-quality monitoring, transition-priority scoring, worker-centered governance documentation, and future-of-work analytics.

## Contents

- `python/task_exposure_job_redesign.py` — task exposure, automation pressure, and augmentation-potential workflow
- `r/job_quality_monitoring.R` — job-quality and transition-priority monitoring workflow
- `sql/workforce_ai_governance_schema.sql` — governance schema for AI labor impact, job quality, training, consultation, and appeals
- `rust/` — command-line task-redesign classifier
- `go/` — lightweight workplace AI governance monitoring service
- `julia/labor_transition_sensitivity.jl` — training and job-quality sensitivity analysis
- `typescript/` — workforce governance record validator
- `cpp/` — high-throughput task-exposure and redesign scoring example
- `docs/templates/` — workforce impact assessment, worker consultation, job-quality monitoring, and appeal templates
- `notebooks/` — notebook placeholder for expanded analysis
- `scripts/run_all.sh` — runs available workflows
