# AI Strategy Governance Checklist

Recommended governance questions:

1. What business problem does the AI initiative address?
2. Is the initiative merely useful or strategically defensible?
3. What proprietary assets does the initiative use?
4. What data rights and governance constraints apply?
5. What workflow changes are required?
6. Who captures the economic surplus?
7. Which competitors can imitate the same use case?
8. Which upstream providers does the initiative depend on?
9. What is the make-buy-partner rationale?
10. What human expertise must be preserved or upgraded?
11. What governance capabilities are required for trust?
12. How will productivity gains be measured?
13. How will value capture be measured?
14. What conditions justify scaling?
15. What conditions justify stopping or retiring the initiative?
