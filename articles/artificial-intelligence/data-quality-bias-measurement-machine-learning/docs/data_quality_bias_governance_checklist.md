# Data Quality, Bias, and Measurement Governance Checklist

Recommended governance questions:

1. What construct is the dataset intended to measure?
2. What proxy variable is used for that construct?
3. Is the proxy conceptually valid?
4. Who or what is represented in the dataset?
5. Who or what is missing?
6. Are labels reliable?
7. Are labels produced consistently across groups?
8. What missingness patterns exist?
9. Is missingness likely MCAR, MAR, or MNAR?
10. Are subgroup sample sizes sufficient?
11. Are error rates evaluated by subgroup?
12. Are historical bias and representation bias documented?
13. Are appropriate and inappropriate uses stated?
14. Is dataset provenance documented?
15. Is there a monitoring plan for drift and degradation?
