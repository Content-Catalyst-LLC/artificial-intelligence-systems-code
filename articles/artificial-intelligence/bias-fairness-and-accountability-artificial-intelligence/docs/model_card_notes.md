# Model Card Notes for Bias, Fairness, and Accountability

Recommended documentation fields:

- system purpose
- decision context
- affected population
- protected attributes reviewed
- training data source
- label source
- known measurement limitations
- known proxy variables
- model family
- threshold policy
- fairness definitions evaluated
- group-level performance metrics
- calibration results by group
- fairness tradeoff justification
- mitigation actions
- human oversight process
- appeal or contestation process
- monitoring plan
- accountability owner
