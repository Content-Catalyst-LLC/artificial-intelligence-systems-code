# Fairness Audit Protocol

Suggested audit sequence:

1. Define decision context.
2. Identify affected populations.
3. Identify relevant protected or sensitive attributes.
4. Document datasets and label sources.
5. Review measurement validity and proxy variables.
6. Compute group-level performance metrics.
7. Compute fairness gaps.
8. Evaluate thresholds and decision rules.
9. Assess calibration by group.
10. Identify mitigation options.
11. Document tradeoffs and decision authority.
12. Confirm human oversight and contestation processes.
13. Define monitoring thresholds.
14. Schedule reassessment.

The audit should be interpreted by domain experts, legal reviewers, governance owners, and affected-stakeholder representatives where appropriate.
