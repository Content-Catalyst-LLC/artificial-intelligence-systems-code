# Bias, Fairness, and Accountability in Artificial Intelligence

This article folder supports the article "Bias, Fairness, and Accountability in Artificial Intelligence" in the Artificial Intelligence Systems knowledge series.

It includes reproducible examples for:

- group fairness metrics
- demographic parity
- equalized odds
- equal opportunity
- predictive parity
- calibration diagnostics
- threshold sweeps
- grouped error analysis
- fairness impact assessment metadata
- SQL metadata for fairness audits and accountability review
- advanced Jupyter notebooks

The examples are educational and synthetic unless otherwise documented.
