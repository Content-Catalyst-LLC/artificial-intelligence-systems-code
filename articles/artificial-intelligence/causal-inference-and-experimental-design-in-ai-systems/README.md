# Causal Inference and Experimental Design in AI Systems

This article folder supports the article "Causal Inference and Experimental Design in AI Systems" in the Artificial Intelligence Systems knowledge series.

It includes reproducible examples for:

- potential outcomes and treatment effects
- randomized experiments and A/B testing
- observational confounding and adjustment
- propensity-score and inverse-probability weighting examples
- heterogeneous treatment effect diagnostics
- causal diagram documentation
- causal governance metadata
- SQL schemas for causal AI experiments and evaluations
- advanced Jupyter notebooks

The examples are educational and synthetic unless otherwise documented.
