# Causal AI Governance Checklist

Recommended governance questions:

1. What is the causal question?
2. What action or treatment is being evaluated?
3. What is the comparison condition?
4. What is the outcome?
5. What is the unit of analysis?
6. What is the estimand?
7. Is treatment assignment randomized?
8. If observational, what confounders must be adjusted for?
9. Are positivity violations possible?
10. Are there multiple versions of treatment?
11. Is interference or spillover likely?
12. Are feedback loops present?
13. Does the effect transport to the deployment environment?
14. What sensitivity analyses were performed?
15. What decisions will the causal estimate support?
