# Future AI Systems Governance Checklist

Recommended governance questions:

1. What AI capabilities are expected to increase?
2. What infrastructure constraints may become binding?
3. Is governance capacity increasing at the same pace as capability?
4. What data constraints affect future deployment?
5. What energy or compute limits shape system design?
6. What human oversight model is required?
7. Which workflows become dependent on AI?
8. What systemic risks could propagate across systems?
9. What platform dependencies exist?
10. Are public-interest and institutional-capacity needs addressed?
11. What evaluation thresholds are required before deployment?
12. What monitoring systems will detect degradation or misuse?
13. What scenario would indicate governance lag?
14. What contingency plans exist for failure or rollback?
15. Who is accountable for future-system review?
