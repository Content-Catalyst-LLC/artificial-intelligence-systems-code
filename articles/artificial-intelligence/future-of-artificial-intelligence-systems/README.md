# The Future of Artificial Intelligence Systems

This article folder supports the article "The Future of Artificial Intelligence Systems: Scaling, Governance, and Sociotechnical Evolution" in the Artificial Intelligence Systems knowledge series.

It includes reproducible examples for:

- simplified scaling-law simulation
- compute and capability tradeoff modeling
- system-fitness scoring
- responsible-scaling gap diagnostics
- future scenario scoring
- infrastructure and governance constraint analysis
- systemic-risk scoring
- SQL schemas for AI futures scenario documentation
- advanced Jupyter notebooks

The examples are educational and synthetic unless otherwise documented.
