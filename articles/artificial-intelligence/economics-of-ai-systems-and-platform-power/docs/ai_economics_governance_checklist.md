# AI Economics and Platform Power Governance Checklist

Recommended governance questions:

1. Which actors control data, compute, cloud, models, distribution, and applications?
2. Where are the bottlenecks?
3. Which dependencies are difficult to substitute?
4. What are the switching costs?
5. Who captures the surplus created by AI?
6. Is value capture shifting upstream?
7. Are downstream firms becoming more dependent?
8. Are model, cloud, and distribution layers vertically integrated?
9. Are interoperability constraints increasing platform power?
10. Are public institutions dependent on private AI infrastructure?
11. What concentration metrics are being monitored?
12. Are procurement strategies reducing or increasing dependence?
13. Are there public-interest compute or data alternatives?
14. What competition-policy risks are visible?
15. What governance actions could preserve contestability?
