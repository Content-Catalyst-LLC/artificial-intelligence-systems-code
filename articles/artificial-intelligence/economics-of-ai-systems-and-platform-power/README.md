# Economics of AI Systems and Platform Power

This article folder supports the article "Economics of AI Systems and Platform Power" in the Artificial Intelligence Systems knowledge series.

It includes reproducible examples for:

- AI value-chain analysis
- platform-power scoring
- market and dependency concentration metrics
- value-capture diagnostics
- compute bottleneck analysis
- dependency and switching-cost scoring
- SQL schemas for AI economics metadata
- governance checklists
- advanced Jupyter notebooks

The examples are educational and synthetic unless otherwise documented.
