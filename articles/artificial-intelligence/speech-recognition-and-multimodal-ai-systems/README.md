# Speech Recognition and Multimodal AI Systems

This article folder supports the article "Speech Recognition and Multimodal AI Systems" in the Artificial Intelligence Systems knowledge series.

It includes reproducible examples for:

- synthetic audio generation
- spectrogram construction
- speech recognition feature pipelines
- CTC alignment intuition
- multimodal embedding similarity
- contrastive learning concepts
- grouped speech error diagnostics
- SQL metadata for multimodal AI systems
- advanced Jupyter notebooks

The examples are educational and synthetic unless otherwise documented.
