# Hybrid AI Governance Checklist

Recommended governance questions:

1. What does the neural component learn?
2. What does the symbolic component represent?
3. Which knowledge base or ontology version is active?
4. Which rules are binding?
5. Which rules are advisory?
6. How are conflicts between neural outputs and symbolic rules resolved?
7. What triggers human review?
8. How are retrieved sources validated?
9. How are rule violations logged?
10. Who owns the symbolic knowledge base?
11. Who owns the neural model?
12. How are updates versioned?
13. How are audit traces stored?
14. How are false symbolic overrides reviewed?
15. How are hallucinated or unsupported outputs prevented?
