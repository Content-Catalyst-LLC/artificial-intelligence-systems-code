# Symbolic AI Governance Checklist

Recommended governance questions:

1. What domain does the knowledge base represent?
2. What classes and relations are included?
3. Which assumptions are encoded in the ontology?
4. Which rules are binding?
5. Which rules are advisory?
6. How are defaults and exceptions handled?
7. How are conflicting rules resolved?
8. What provenance supports each fact?
9. Who owns rule maintenance?
10. Who approves ontology changes?
11. How are inference traces stored?
12. What conclusions require human review?
13. How are outdated facts deprecated?
14. How is symbolic knowledge connected to neural or statistical components?
15. How are errors, gaps, and disputes corrected?
