# Knowledge Representation and Symbolic AI Systems

This article folder supports the article "Knowledge Representation and Symbolic AI Systems" in the Artificial Intelligence Systems knowledge series.

It includes reproducible examples for:

- symbolic facts
- semantic triples
- rule-based inference
- knowledge graph tables
- ontology-like schemas
- inference traces
- rule coverage diagnostics
- AI governance metadata
- SQL schemas for symbolic AI systems
- advanced Jupyter notebooks

The examples are educational and synthetic unless otherwise documented.
