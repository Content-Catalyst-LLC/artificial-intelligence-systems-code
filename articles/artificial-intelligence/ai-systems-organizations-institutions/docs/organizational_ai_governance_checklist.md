# Organizational AI Governance Checklist

Recommended governance questions:

1. What organizational decision or workflow does the AI system affect?
2. Who owns the AI use case?
3. Who is affected by the decision?
4. What decision mode is appropriate: support, review, supervision, or automation?
5. What is the harm potential?
6. Does the system affect rights, safety, employment, benefits, healthcare, education, finance, or public services?
7. Is the decision reversible?
8. Is the system interpretable enough for the workflow?
9. Is meaningful human review possible?
10. Can users override or contest the AI output?
11. Are model and dataset documentation complete?
12. Are subgroup and fairness impacts monitored?
13. Are staff trained to use the system responsibly?
14. Are incidents tracked and reviewed?
15. Is the system aligned with institutional mission, legal duties, and professional norms?
