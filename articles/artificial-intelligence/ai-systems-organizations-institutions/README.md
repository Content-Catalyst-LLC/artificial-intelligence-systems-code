# AI Systems in Organizations and Institutions

This article folder supports the article "AI Systems in Organizations and Institutions" in the Artificial Intelligence Systems knowledge series.

It includes reproducible examples for:

- organizational AI readiness scoring
- human-AI decision allocation
- workflow risk diagnostics
- institutional governance maturity scoring
- public-sector AI risk review
- decision-mode recommendation
- SQL schemas for organizational AI governance
- advanced Jupyter notebooks

The examples are educational and synthetic unless otherwise documented.
