# Systemic Risk, Feedback Loops, and Cascading Failures in AI Systems

This article folder supports the article "Systemic Risk, Feedback Loops, and Cascading Failures in AI Systems" in the Artificial Intelligence Systems knowledge series.

It includes reproducible examples for:

- dependency-network modeling
- cascade propagation simulation
- feedback-loop diagnostics
- provider concentration scoring
- coupling and buffer analysis
- systemic-risk scoring
- SQL schemas for systemic AI risk metadata
- governance checklists
- advanced Jupyter notebooks

The examples are educational and synthetic unless otherwise documented.
