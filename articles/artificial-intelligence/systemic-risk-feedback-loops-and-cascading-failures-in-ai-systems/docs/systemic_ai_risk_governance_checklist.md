# Systemic AI Risk Governance Checklist

Recommended governance questions:

1. What upstream providers does the system depend on?
2. What downstream workflows depend on this system?
3. Are any dependencies common across multiple organizational units?
4. Are there single points of failure?
5. Are feedback loops documented?
6. Are self-reinforcing loops monitored?
7. Can local errors propagate automatically?
8. How tightly coupled are the workflows?
9. Are fallback modes available?
10. Are rollback procedures tested?
11. Are incident propagation paths logged?
12. Are affected teams notified when failures propagate?
13. Is provider concentration reviewed?
14. Are cascade scenarios simulated?
15. Who owns cross-system remediation?
