# Human-AI Interface Design Checklist

1. What task does the AI support?
2. Who are the direct users?
3. Who is affected by the system?
4. What should users know before relying on the output?
5. How are uncertainty and scope limits communicated?
6. What explanation is appropriate for each user role?
7. What could cause overreliance?
8. What could cause underreliance?
9. Can users override the system?
10. Can users escalate uncertain or high-risk cases?
11. Can affected people contest outcomes?
12. Is the interface accessible?
13. Does the system log outputs, explanations, and user actions?
14. Has the interface been tested under realistic workload?
15. Who owns remediation when interaction design contributes to harm?
