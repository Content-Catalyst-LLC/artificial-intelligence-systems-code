# User-Centered AI Design Checklist

Recommended design questions:

1. Who are the direct users?
2. Who is affected by the system?
3. What decision or task does the AI support?
4. What does the user need to know before relying on the output?
5. What uncertainty should be shown?
6. What explanation type is appropriate for each user role?
7. What could cause overreliance?
8. What could cause underreliance?
9. What should trigger human review?
10. Can users override the system?
11. Can affected people contest outcomes?
12. Is the interface accessible?
13. Has the system been tested with representative users?
14. Are user interactions logged for monitoring?
15. Who owns remediation when the system causes harm?
