# Decision Support Governance Checklist

## Purpose

- Decision question is documented.
- Intended use is documented.
- Prohibited use is documented.
- Decision authority is assigned.

## Prediction

- Prediction target is defined.
- Calibration is reviewed.
- Uncertainty is reported.
- Distribution shift is monitored.

## Decision Logic

- Utility function is documented.
- Constraints are documented.
- Thresholds are approved.
- Scenario assumptions are reviewed.

## Human Oversight

- Human review rules are defined.
- Override process is documented.
- Override reasons are logged.
- Contestability process is defined.

## Governance

- Data lineage is preserved.
- Model version is recorded.
- Decision logs are auditable.
- Monitoring cadence is defined.
- Accountability is assigned.
