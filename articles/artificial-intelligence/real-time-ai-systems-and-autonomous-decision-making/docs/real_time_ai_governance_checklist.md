# Real-Time AI Governance Checklist

Recommended governance questions:

1. What decisions are time-critical?
2. What are the hard, firm, or soft deadlines?
3. What is the end-to-end latency budget?
4. Which component dominates latency?
5. What is the deadline-miss rate?
6. What is the worst-case latency?
7. What is the p95 or p99 latency?
8. What jitter is acceptable?
9. Are safety monitors scheduled at sufficient priority?
10. What fallback action is used when timing fails?
11. Can humans pause or override the autonomous system?
12. Is edge inference required for resilience?
13. Are runtime assurance events logged?
14. Are incidents reviewed after deployment?
15. Who owns timing, safety, and autonomy governance?
